Lord of Mysteries

It's nice, trust me

By technicality this is an isekei story but it's Chinese so it's actually really in-depth.
The novel is anything but light and, as of writing this, the current 65 chapters of the manhua only cover 2/3 of the first of eight volumes comprising the first book.
Along the same lines, the comic is much more summarised than the novel. If you still want a correspondance, manhua ch65 = novel vol1ch104.

Manhua
