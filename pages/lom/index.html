Lord of Mysteries

It's nice, trust me

Chinese isekai, if you want to be reductive about it. Also the greatest book I've ever read
The novel is anything but light, but the payoff of having the time to build everything up and coming to realizations about the scale of it all is worth all 1432 chapters of it.
