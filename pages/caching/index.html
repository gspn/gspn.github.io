Caching

What you need to know about running these pages offline

Cache icons and tags

These little pucks tell you the status of a page in cache:

  • UNTRACKED means the page isn't in cache
  • FRESH means the page has been cached recently (less than a day old)
  • STALE means you should re-cache if you're expecting changes (less than a week old)
  • OLD means you should definitely re-cache it, unless you're certain nothing important has changed (older than a week)
  • ERROR means the cache update failed in any way. This is most commonly just trying to update cache while offline.
You can click the cachetag to attempt an update of the cache for the associated file.
